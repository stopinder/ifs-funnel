Privacy Policy

Chrysalis Therapy Services values your privacy and protects your data under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR).

1. What Information Is Collected?

We may collect your name, contact information, date of birth, health-related notes, and communications relevant to therapy sessions and services.

2. Why We Collect Data

To provide psychotherapy, generate reflective summaries, manage scheduling, and fulfill legal/professional obligations.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

Data is processed under consent, contract, and legitimate interest. Health-related data is handled under Article 9(2)(h) of the UK GDPR (health care provision).

4. AI-Assisted Summaries

Reflective summaries may use AI tools (e.g. GPT-based models). These are therapist-supervised, and no identifying data is shared with third parties. All outputs are reviewed and finalised by the therapist.

5. Data Retention

Records are kept securely for up to 7 years after the conclusion of therapy, or longer where legally required.

6. Your Rights

You may request to access, amend, or erase your personal data. You may also object to its processing or request portability, in accordance with GDPR.
